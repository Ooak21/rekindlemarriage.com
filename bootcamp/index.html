Good questions

Before you reserve

Do we have to be struggling to join?

Not at all. Rekindle is built for couples who are doing okay and want to be great, as much as for couples who feel stuck. It is proactive by design.

Is this therapy?

No. Rekindle is education and coaching in a small group setting, not clinical treatment or diagnosis. The curriculum is developed alongside licensed marriage therapists, and if deeper issues surface, we help you find the right licensed support.

How much time does it take?

The bootcamp runs four to six weeks, with a session every two weeks. Each session is about 30 to 60 minutes of teaching plus discussion and activities, with light to do assignments between sessions.

What does it cost, and do we both attend?

It is $600 per couple for the full program, and yes, you attend together. That covers every session, your at home toolkit, and your invitation to the six month follow up.

What happens after we reserve?

You get an instant confirmation to both emails, your seat is held, and you complete enrollment with a secure payment. Then we send your cohort schedule and welcome details.
